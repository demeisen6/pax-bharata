# The Mongol Divergence

An alternate history of the Pax Bharata world: a contained Mongol Empire, a
surviving Abbasid Caliphate, a China of a hundred kingdoms, and an age of
colonization driven from the Indian subcontinent instead of Western Europe.

**Read it here:** https://demeisen6.github.io/pax-bharata/

This world is the setting of *Pax Bharata*, a scenario mod for
[Terra Invicta](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1176470/Terra_Invicta/).
The mod download will be posted here after playtesting.
